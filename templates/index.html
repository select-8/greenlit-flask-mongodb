GREENLIT PRODUCTIONS

Everyone has brilliant story to tell.

  • This site was designed to give you the oppertunity to pitch that story and if you are lucky, have it Greenlit for production by us.

  • If you are not yet a member, feel free to browse current Pitches. Then if you think you have what it takes, come back and Register.

  • What can a member do?
  • Think of it as your two minutes in an elevator with one of our producers.

  • Frist, you will want to grab their attention by providing an awesome TITLE.

  • Next, what GENRE of movie is it? who do you see playing the LEAD? who do you want to DIRECT?
  • We have a roster of top Hollywood talent for you to choose from.

  • Of course you will need to give a short DESCRIPTION of the plot for you movie. Remember, your in an elevator, so keep it snappy!

  • Finally, and in the end we are going to have to sell your movie to audiences. To do this we need a TAGLINE.

  • You can add as many pitches as you like
  • If you are not successful on your first submission you will have three changes to make edits and resubmitt.
  • Once all your edits are gone you can get another chance to submitt if enough of our other members vote for it.
